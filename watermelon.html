I Love Watermelon

When I think about my favorite treat

All my tastebuds start to get excited

Temptations so delightfull, why resist?

Eating my favorite snack in the whole world

Realy makes my day better in so many ways

Melting on my tongue chunk and cube

Encasing everything in juices so sweet

Licking delighted chops somehow not yet satisfied

Only six slices left to go, what will it hurt?

Now what is this way too full feeling? Oh...REGRET.
